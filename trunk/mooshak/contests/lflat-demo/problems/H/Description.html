Problem: First (LR)

Consider the following grammar
    S -> E
    E -> e | pF;
    F -> E | EF
Now create the corresponding deterministic finite automaton of the valid items LR(1) and then build a response firstANS for the following five questions:
  • How many states does the automaton have?
  • In how many states does LR(0) conflicts occur?
  • Is the grammar LR(0)?
  • Is the grammar LR(1)?
  • Is the grammar LALR(1)?
